Transcend Madison

innovation competition

A cross-collaborative competition showcasing student innovations.
All you need is an idea to compete—all you need is hustle to win.

What: Show off your innovations for a chance at $20,000 in prizes
When: March 10-11 | Where: Union South

Introducing the Transcend Madison competition, hosted by Transcend Engineering - a registered student organization at UW-Madison

Our goal is to bring students from all across UW Madison to demonstrate their novel ideas and creations. Unlike previous competitions, anyone can compete—all you need is an idea.

However, this competition will combine all aspects of entrepreneurialism, so a winning idea exhibits:

  • a novel idea
  • a valid prototype
  • a tested business model
  • and most importantly, great execution
